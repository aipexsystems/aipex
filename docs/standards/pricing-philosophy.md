# AIPEX Pricing Philosophy

## Core Principle

We do not sell hours.

We sell:

- Revenue growth
- Operational efficiency
- Risk reduction
- Time savings
- Customer trust
- Competitive advantage

## Value Creation Doctrine

Every engagement must produce:

- Reusable intellectual property
- Standard operating procedures
- Documentation
- Product opportunities
- Recurring revenue pathways

## Revenue Hierarchy

Project Revenue
↓
Managed Services
↓
Platform Revenue
↓
Subscription Revenue

Projects are temporary.

Retainers endure.

Products compound.

## Pricing Framework

Price according to:

- Economic value created
- Strategic importance
- Operational complexity
- Risk transferred
- Speed to outcome

Never price according to:

- Hours worked
- Lines of code
- Technology stacks
- Developer effort

## Engagement Philosophy

Sell outcomes.

Document everything.

Productize recurring solutions.

Build ecosystems, not one-off projects.

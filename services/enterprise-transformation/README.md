# Enterprise Transformation Program

Status: Active
Version: 1.0

## Purpose

Redesign organizational systems, processes, and technology foundations to support sustainable growth, operational excellence, and long-term scalability.

This engagement addresses structural bottlenecks rather than isolated problems.

---

## Ideal Customer

Organizations that:

- Have outgrown existing systems.
- Operate with disconnected departments.
- Struggle with executive visibility.
- Experience process inefficiencies.
- Require modernization initiatives.

Employee Range:

50–500+ employees.

Primary Industries:

- Construction
- Senior Living
- Home Healthcare

---

## Business Outcomes

Clients achieve:

- Greater organizational scalability.
- Unified operational visibility.
- Reduced administrative complexity.
- Improved decision-making.
- Stronger governance and accountability.
- Lower technology risk.
- Faster execution across departments.

## Qualification Requirements

Clients should:

- Have executive sponsorship.
- Commit to organizational change.
- Designate internal leaders.
- Support documentation initiatives.
- Possess measurable strategic objectives.

Disqualifiers:

- Technology-first thinking without business alignment.
- Lack of leadership commitment.
- Undefined success criteria.
- Resistance to operational transparency.

---

## Discovery Questions

### Leadership

- What prevents scaling today?
- Which decisions are difficult to make?
- What information is missing?

### Operations

- Which processes create friction?
- Where are delays introduced?
- Which departments operate in isolation?

### Technology

- What systems no longer support growth?
- Which integrations are missing?
- What security concerns exist?

### Strategy

- What does success look like in three years?
- Which capabilities must the organization develop?

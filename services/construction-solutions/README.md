# Construction Solutions

Status: Active
Version: 1.0

## Purpose

Modernize construction operations through integrated digital systems that improve project delivery, communication, visibility, and profitability.

AIPEX helps construction firms replace fragmented tools and manual processes with scalable operational ecosystems.

---

## Ideal Customer

Organizations that:

- Manage multiple active projects.
- Depend on spreadsheets and manual coordination.
- Experience communication breakdowns.
- Lack executive visibility.
- Need better lead-to-project workflows.

Employee Range:

10–250+ employees.

Primary Customers:

- General Contractors
- Specialty Contractors
- Residential Builders
- Commercial Builders
- Construction Management Firms

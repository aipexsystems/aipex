# Discovery Questions

Business:

- What prevents growth today?
- How are leads currently managed?
- Where do customers become frustrated?
- Which manual tasks consume the most time?

Technology:

- What software do you currently use?
- What systems fail most frequently?
- What information is difficult to access?

Leadership:

- What does success look like in twelve months?
- Which metrics matter most?

# Senior Living Solutions

Status: Active
Version: 1.0

## Purpose

Create compassionate, secure, and efficient digital ecosystems that improve resident experiences, empower staff, and simplify operations.

Technology should reduce administrative burden so caregivers can focus on people.

---

## Ideal Customer

Organizations that:

- Operate assisted living facilities.
- Manage multiple locations.
- Struggle with staff communication.
- Depend on manual administrative processes.
- Need stronger digital trust.

Employee Range:

20–500+ employees.

Primary Customers:

- Assisted Living Facilities
- RCFEs
- Independent Living Communities
- Memory Care Organizations
- Senior Care Operators

---

## Business Outcomes

Clients achieve:

- Better resident experiences.
- Improved staff coordination.
- Reduced administrative workload.
- Stronger family communication.
- Increased occupancy.
- Improved operational visibility.
- Greater regulatory preparedness.

## Discovery Questions

### Residents & Families

- What feedback is most common?
- How are families updated?
- Where does communication fail?

### Staff

- What administrative tasks consume time?
- Which systems create frustration?
- What training challenges exist?

### Operations

- How is occupancy tracked?
- What reports are manual?
- Which processes create bottlenecks?

### Leadership

- Which metrics matter most?

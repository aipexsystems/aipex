# Managed Services Retainers

Status: Active
Version: 1.0

## Purpose

Provide continuous optimization, maintenance, security, and strategic guidance that protects client investments and enables long-term growth.

This engagement transforms completed projects into enduring partnerships.

---

## Ideal Customer

Organizations that:

- Have completed Digital Foundation engagements.
- Have completed Growth Operations initiatives.
- Have undergone enterprise transformation.
- Require ongoing technical leadership.
- Value proactive support over reactive fixes.

Employee Range:

10–500+ employees.

Primary Industries:

- Construction
- Senior Living
- Home Healthcare

---

## Business Outcomes

Clients achieve:

- Reduced operational risk.
- Predictable technology costs.
- Continuous improvement.
- Stronger security posture.
- Better executive visibility.
- Faster issue resolution.
- Sustainable organizational growth.

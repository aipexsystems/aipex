# CTA Principles

Every CTA should:

✓ Focus on partnership.

✓ Reduce pressure.

✓ Encourage conversation.

✓ Emphasize outcomes.

✓ Sound professional and confident.

---

# Avoid CTAs Like

✗ Buy Now

✗ Get Started Today!!!

✗ Claim Your Spot

✗ Limited Time Offer

✗ Free Consultation

✗ Click Here

✗ Contact Us Now

---

# AIPEX Conversion Doctrine

We do not chase leads.

We begin conversations.

We do not sell technology.

We solve business problems.

We do not create customers.

We build long-term partners.
